About Extended Support

What Is Extended Support ?

Beyond the confines of legal proceedings, Zipporah Law Firm extends a caring hand to provide holistic support to our clients. Recognizing the emotional and practical challenges that often accompany legal matters, we offer a range of extended support services designed to address diverse needs. From providing access to counselling services for emotional well-being, facilitating medical assistance for healthcare needs, ensuring child security in sensitive situations, to offering career guidance for those undergoing transitions, our holistic approach aims to alleviate burdens and empower individuals to navigate life's challenges with resilience and confidence.

 
OUR SERVICE

We Offer a Wide

Variety of Extended Support

Counselling

Our counselling services provide a confidential space for emotional support and guidance, helping you navigate life's challenges with resilience and clarity.

Career Guidance

Our career guidance services offer tailored advice and resources to help you chart a path forward with confidence and purpose, even amidst legal complexities.

Child Security

We prioritize child security with dedicated measures, ensuring a safe and nurturing environment for the young ones impacted by legal proceedings.

Medical Assistance

Access timely medical assistance through our network of trusted healthcare professionals, ensuring your well-being remains a top priority in your legal journey.

Crisis Support

Emotional support and guidance during times of crisis or personal difficulty, including referrals to counseling services if needed.

Community Resources

Referrals to community organizations and resources for assistance with housing, food insecurity, legal aid, and other social services.
